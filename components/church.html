Church Transformation

  • by:Avery Chen
  • date:2021
  • Community Management / Live Broadcast / Visual Design

The project is about the transformation of the church. The church was founded by a young priest, Peter. His idea is to operate this church, The Hope, in an innovative method. The Hope should have expaned the size faster, but during the Covid pandemic, he decided to transform. Thus, he recruited volunteers. I was lucky enough to join the team. Our team was in charge of operating the social media platforms, visual designs and videos. The transformation increased more than 10k followers, which attracted more than 1.3 millions this year.

Online Channels

Apart from the online stream channels, we run YouTube channels, including various topics and content.
The most popular topic is “Pastor, I want to ask”. This is a channel to interact with followers or belivers. They can inquire questions via Facebook or Intagram. We will answer and discuss the questions on the Internet.

Promotional video for the online program “Pastor, I want to ask”

Management on social media

Pastors led us to visual team. We worked on social media and visual designs, recording our positive and dynamic events.

Most prayers of the church are young and lost people. Thus, it is very important to visualize the lecture to demonstrate the topic and the content. At the special events, the series of designs will be shown as films and lights which will be posted on social media for prayers to download as wallpaper.

Activities

Visual design is extremely important for the event. In addition to the sound and light effect requirements for the layout of the event, there are also advertising videos and graphic design in advance.

Growth of this year

It is seen that the information of the growth before this year and after this year on The Hope website.
There are more than 3000 people attending our events every Sunday, which creates 20% increase over last year. Moreover, online participation rate is 60%.

The achievements of the transformation from onsite to online are included from broadcasts to YouTube shows. In total, there are over 1,450,000 times watched.

Financial records this year and the amount raised during the year.
This year's transformation not only allowed the church to survive during the pandemic, but also achieved more significant growth and popularity among lost belivers.
